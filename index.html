Recording Stats Made Easy

Track Easily

With Footykeeper, creating your roster and recording player performances is as easy as 1-2-3! Just input your roster, give the converted roster string to the tracker, and record player events! You can then print out a table containing the players' ratings, goals, assists, and much more!

Footykeeper is deigned for anybody interested in improving the method of recording their team's statistics. Footykeepers system allows you to record:

  • Recordable Events

  • Goals
  • Assists
  • Key Passes
  • Shot on Target
  • Successful Dribbles
  • Successful Crosses
  • dispossessions
  • Own Goals
  • Saves
  • Interceptions
  • Tackles Won
  • Penalties Saved
  • Yellow Cards
  • Second Yellows
  • Red Cards
  • Aerials Won
  • Effective Clearances
